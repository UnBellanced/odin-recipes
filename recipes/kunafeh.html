Kunafeh

Description

Knafeh is a traditional Middle Eastern dessert, made with spun pastry called kataifi, soaked in a sweet, sugar-based syrup called attar.

Layered with cheese, or with other ingredients such as clotted cream, pistachio or nuts.

Ingridients

  • Cheese
  • Sugar
  • Lemon
  • Kataifi pastry

    • Steps

    Spin the pastry

    Make the syrup
