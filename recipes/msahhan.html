Msahhan

Description

Musakhan, also known as muhammar, is a Palestinian dish starring roasted chicken.

Baked with onions, sumac, allspice, saffron and fried pine nuts served over taboon bread.

Ingridients

  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Taboon Bread

    • Steps
